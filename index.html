Project X

A 2D platform side-scroller video game. It was developed in Unity using C# with MonoDevelop IDE.

Introduction

Team Elk developed Project X for CS 113: Computer Game Developer, Winter 2014 at University of California, Irvine.

Gameplay

The goal is to reach the end of the stage. There are robot enemies that will damage the player on touch and portals that teleport the player to random locations in the stage. Throughout the game, there are powerups that modify the player's weapons: gun and sword.

Weapons

The player starts out with a ranged projectile gun and a melee sword.

Sword Powerup

Here, the player has picked up a powerup that increases the range and damage of the player's sword.

Gun Powerup

Here, the player has picked up a powerup that increases the damage of the player's gun and consequently, changing the projectile's color to a green tint.

In this scene, the player sees a green powerup that upgrades the player's gun to fire icy projectiles.

The player fires the new icy projectiles into a large robot, slowing the robot's movement.

Boss Portal

At the end of the game, there is a portal that leads to the boss level.

Boss Level

After entering the large black portal, the player fights the large robot boss.
